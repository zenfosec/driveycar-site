Support

Drivey Car is an arcade game for iPhone, iPad, and Apple Watch, made by Satoru Labs. It's free to play, with no ads.

Frequently asked

How do I use the optional Game Center leaderboard?
Leaderboards use Apple's Game Center, and sign-in is managed by iOS; open Settings → Game Center on your device and sign in there (for Apple Watch, sign in on the paired iPhone). It's entirely optional; the game is fully playable without it.
Where is my progress stored?
On your device. Drivey Car needs no account and no sign-up; your progress and settings are stored on the device itself, per device. If you delete the app, that information is deleted with it.
Does Drivey Car collect my data?
No. No ads, no accounts, no analytics, no tracking. See the privacy policy; it's a two-minute read.
How do I restore a purchase?
If you've made a purchase in Drivey Car, open Settings in the app and tap Restore Purchases. Purchases are tied to your Apple Account, so signing in with the same account restores them on your other devices.
Something isn't working. What should I do?
Email us. Include your device model and OS version, and describe what happened. We read everything.
